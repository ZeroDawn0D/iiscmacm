    About Us IISc ACM student chapter

    IISc has been making significant progress in Computer Science, with consistent representation in many top-tier conferences and high impact journals. The institute hosts research groups dealing with a diverse array of problems that range from theoretical computer science and computer systems, to computational data sciences. These research groups are spread across various departments. Yet, there does not exist a unified platform for researchers to exchange ideas, interact with professionals, and estimate the practical impact of their work.

    IISc’s ACM Student Chapter will aim at providing an effective platform for:

    • Highlighting and recognizing high quality research from within the institute
    • Building better communication channels with alumni from industry and academia
    • Bridging the gap between research and practice

    The Chapter aspires to create a connected community of computer science researchers within the institute while maintaining industry collaborations if they serve research interests. The Chapter, in it's first year of operation intends on organizing the following events/activities:

    Career Counsel
    The Chapter intends on hosting regular formal or informal interactions with alumni in academia and industry. Assisting students in making an informed career decision is the broad agenda of these interactions.
    Dress Rehearsals for Presentations
    Upon request from Chapter members, the Chapter can arrange a suitable platform for authors of accepted papers to present their work prior to the Conference presentation and gain practice and confidence to effectively present their paper at the venue. Chapter members must contact the Student Representative for the Chapter to make suitable arrangements.
    Annual Student Get-together
    The Chapter will organize an annual get-together for it’s chapter member with the sole purpose of improving comradery.
    Chapter Newsletter
    The Chapter’s quarterly newsletter is a primary instrument to achieve a connected community of budding and established researchers. The newsletter must cover topics pertaining to the latest research by students of IISc, upcoming fellowship opportunities, and occasionally computer science trivia.

    Lighthouse Goals (LEO)
    Leadership
    The Chapter must take active steps towards cultivating and identifying leadership qualities among its members. This will help the Chapter in maintaining a steady flow of Chapter Officers and volunteers while also helping the community by producing effective leaders.
    Events
    The Chapter Officers are encouraged to add events to its roster. However the events must: benefit IISc’s computer science community, align with IISc’s research spirit, and strictly foster scientific thinking.
    Outreach
    The Chapter must work towards increasing its outreach. The Chapter must emerge as a flag-bearer in the region and assist other local Chapters in their operation.
    About Us

    The IISc ACM Student Chapter is a joint initiative by the research students from Department of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS) and Computer Science and Automation (CSA). The chapter aims to further communication channels with alumni from academia and industry, highlight and recognize research within the institute and collectively bridge gap between research and practice.
